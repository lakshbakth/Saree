Our Story

Preserving India's
Living Heritage

Saree Story was born from a simple belief: that every handwoven saree deserves to be found, appreciated, and worn. We travel to weaving clusters across India — Kanchipuram, Varanasi, Chanderi, Patan — and work directly with weavers.

No middlemen. No compromises. Just honest craft, documented and celebrated.
